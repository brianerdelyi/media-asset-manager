Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for videographers and media professionals who work with large libraries across multiple drives.

Fast search
Search across your entire library instantly. Filter by media type, drive, status, and markers.
Drive management
Register any drive or folder. Online/offline detection happens automatically when you plug in.
Video playback
Preview your footage inline. Scrub the timeline, set In/Out points, and export lossless clips.
Markers
Add point and range markers to any asset. Click to seek. Export range markers as lossless clips.
Local-first
SQLite database on your Mac. No cloud sync, no subscriptions, no sending your data anywhere.
Light & dark mode
Follows your macOS system preference automatically. Override to light or dark in Settings.
Built with

Modern, native stack.

Tauri keeps the binary small and the app fast. React powers the UI. Rust handles everything that needs to be reliable.
